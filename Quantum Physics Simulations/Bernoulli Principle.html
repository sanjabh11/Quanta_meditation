Bernoulli Principle

The Bernoulli Principle states that an increase in the speed of a fluid occurs simultaneously with a decrease in pressure or a decrease in the fluid's potential energy.

p + ½ρv² + ρgh = constant

Where:

  • p is pressure
  • ρ (rho) is density
  • v is velocity
  • g is gravitational acceleration
  • h is height

In the Animation:

  • Particles represent fluid (air or water)
  • The shape represents an airfoil or constriction
  • Particle color indicates pressure (red: high, blue: low)
  • Particle speed is shown by its movement
  • Streamlines visualize the flow pattern
